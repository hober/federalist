Colophon

From the blog post explaining why I built this site:

Of course, there are various webbed versions of the Federalist Papers on the Internet already[…] Unfortunately, I haven’t been able find one that was simultaneously nice-looking and useful[…] So I went ahead and made[…] an online edition of the Federalist Papers which is pleasant to look at and provides paragraph-level permalinking.

Type

I set federali.st in Goudy Bookletter 1911, a lovely, public domain typeface made avaiable by The Crud Factory. I converted Goudy Bookletter 1911 from .otf to .eot with FontForge and ttf2eot as described in How to create EOT files without Microsoft WEFT.
